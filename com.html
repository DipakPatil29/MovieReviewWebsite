FREE GUY

Release Date: Aug 13, 2021

Director: Shawn Levy

Rating: 8.4/10

In "Free Guy," a bank teller who discovers he is actually a background player in an open-world video game, decides to become the hero of his own story... one he rewrites himself. Now in a world where there are no limits, he is determined to be the guy who saves his world his way... before it is too late.

KUNG FU PANDA

Release Date: Jun 6, 2008

Director: John Stevenson, Mark Osborne

Rating: 9.2/10

Po the panda (Jack Black) works in his family's noodle shop and dreams of becoming a kung-fu master. His dream becomes a reality when, unexpectedly, he must fulfill an ancient prophecy and study the skills with his idols, the Furious Five. Po needs all the wisdom, strength and ability he can muster to protect his people from an evil snow leopard.
